# Design System: RealERPCRM

## 1. Brand Identity
**Name:** RealERPCRM
**Tagline:** Smart. Fast. Reliable.

## 2. Color Palette
- **Primary (Indigo):** `#4F46E5` (Main brand color, buttons, accents)
- **Secondary (Charcoal):** `#111827` (Headings, heavy text)
- **Accent (Amber):** `#F59E0B` (Important notices, badges)
- **Background:** `#fafbfc` (Clean, modern workspace)
- **Card/Surface:** `#ffffff` (White with subtle slate-100 borders)

## 3. Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter, system-ui (Modern, clean sans-serif)
- **Heading Font:** Inter (Extra Bold, Italic for emphasis)
- **Styles:** Use `italic` for secondary descriptions to match the brand's fast-moving feel.

## 4. UI Patterns
- **Cards:** Large border radius (`2.5rem` or `3rem`), thin borders (`1px border-slate-100`), subtle shadow.
- **Buttons:** Fully rounded (`rounded-full`), uppercase, bold, letter-spacing tracking-widest.
- **Layout:** High whitespace, centered containers, premium spacing.

## 5. Components
- **Navbar:** Top info bar with contact info, sticky main nav with glass effect.
- **Footer:** Deep footer with logo, contact column, link columns, and partner logos.
